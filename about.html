Business Description

Car Parts Store is a for-profit online retail business selling OEM and aftermarket parts for popular brands (Honda, Toyota, Subaru, Mazda, Mitsubishi, Suzuki, BMW). We offer parts grouped by brand, model, part type and year for easy selection.

Logo / Slogan / Mission / Vision

Slogan: "Parts you trust, shipped fast."

Mission: To supply high-quality, affordable car parts to mechanics and enthusiasts across Jamaica with fast delivery and excellent customer support.

Vision: To become the premier online car parts supplier in Jamaica and the Caribbean.
